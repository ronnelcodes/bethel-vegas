About Our Church

Pastor Chris and Denmark grew up in Boulder City, NV. In 1999 they moved to Beaumont, CA and served on staff at a church for 14 years. Towards the end of 2012, they felt God was leading them to transition the church that Chris was then pastoring and move back to Las Vegas to start a new church in the. They envisioned a place so filled with life that you couldnâ€™t wait to get there. A place that would help people from every generation and every walk of life discover all that God has for them. That vision became a reality with the launch of Bethel Vegas on January 19th, 2014. From the beginning, they have seen a major move of God. In the 3 years they have been in Vegas, lives are being changed though life in Christ week after week. People are coming to know God, grow in community, discover purpose and make a difference in the world around them.
